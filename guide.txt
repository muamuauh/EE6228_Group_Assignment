Group Project on Process Modeling and Scheduling



1. Each group consists of up to 5 students. 

2. Each member of a group must submit a duplicated copy of the group report either as a DOC file or a PDF file. 

3. Each report should include the following contents:

(1) Introduction to the use case - what is about?

(2) A mathematical formulation of your scheduling problem.

(3) A solution strategy with necessary tool descriptions (you are free to use any solvers that you can find).

(4) Your solution and discussion 

(5) A simulation demo (optional - will give bonus marks)

(6) References



Please keep your use case not too complicated.
